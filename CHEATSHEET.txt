OPENING LINE
  "This is an AI agent for interview feedback. The twist: the AI never
   makes the hiring decision. Let me show you how it's boxed in."

THE ONE IDEA (repeat it at the end)
  Decisions stay in deterministic code. The LLM only writes the
  explanation, from pre-filtered data. So a bad scorecard can't change
  a decision or leak into the output.
